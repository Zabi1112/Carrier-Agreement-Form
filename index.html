Freight Dispatch Agreement

This Freight Dispatch Agreement, (the "Agreement") is effective on the 10th day of June 2025,
by and between: GET MOVING LOGISTICS (hereinafter known as "Dispatch" or "Dispatcher")
NCT LOGISTICS INC(hereinafter known as "Carrier").
WHEREAS, the Dispatch, desires to enter into an agreement with Carrier for Freight Dispatch.
WHEREAS, the Carrier, a licensed interstate carrier of property holding authority by the FMCSA,
with MC # 1430967 agrees to enter into an agreement with the Dispatch herein.
WHEREAS, the Dispatch and the Carrier agree to the following terms and conditions:
DOCUMENTATION
Client must furnish to Get Moving Logistics LLC the documents mentioned below:
MC Authority Letter
Certificate of Insurance
W-9 From
NOA (Notice of assignment) which is your factoring document.

CARRIER RESPONSIBILITIES
Carrier shall be responsible in all the necessary documentary and legal compliance with its
operations as a carrier and assumes to be legally liable for any loss, damage, delay, destruction,
theft, or any liability of whatever nature arises from the transportation of any freight as contemplated
and falling under this agreement. Carrier shall perform the transport diligently as arranged by the
Dispatch and as accepted by the Carrier and shall ensure that all equipment necessary to the
compliance to state laws are properly complied with. Carrier shall assume taxes, premiums, and
other benefits under the employ of the Carrier and shall maintain the minimum qualifications of
insurance as prescribed by the FMCSA.

FEE
The Carrier agrees to pay the Dispatcher 3% of the loads that the Dispatcher has provided as part
of their service. The payment will be charged to the Carrier on a weekly basis. Also Carrier will 
provide 3 DAT load boards to Dispatcher.
RELATIONSHIP & RESPONSIBILITIES
The relationship between the parties shall be of an independent contractor. Dispatch shall offer
freight transportation shipments to Carrier to reasonable or acceptable locations as may be required,
subject to the availability of the Carrier.
Dispatch shall:
Find freight that best matches profile for the Client and negotiate pay rates with the
shipper/broker wherever necessary.
Credit checks the broker
Route planning
Arrange fuel advances wherever possible or if requested

Contact Client with load matches and go over options.
Fax/text/e-mail to shipper/broker the Client’s MC Authority, W-9, proof of insurance, and order
insurance certificates, if required, along with any other required supporting documentation upon
the Client agreeing to take a load.
Handle the setting of appointments if necessary.
Provide the driver with all dispatch instructions for pickup, transit and delivery.
Assist with any problems that arise in the transit of the load when necessary if within our
capabilities. The Client is responsible for own equipment. We can try to direct Client to a service
that might be of help.
Hold on to the dispatch, accessorial information, etc. until the load is completed. Once
completed -Forward the final load confirmation and mail all documentation to the Client,
concluding that all services have been performed in full.
TERMS
This agreement starts on the date both parties sign it and will be in effect for one year. After that, it
will renew automatically each year.
TERMINATION
Either party can end this agreement by giving a written notice to the other party at least 7 days
before the termination takes effect.
BILLS OF LADING
Each freight must constitute a bill of lading which shall be issued by the broker or shipper.
DAMAGES
The Party found to be responsible for any loss, damage, and damages, resulting from negligence or
delay shall be liable for any unless such fault is not attributable to said Carrier.
NON-ASSIGNMENT
The Carrier agrees that all freight tendered by the Dispatch to the Carrier shall not be assigned,
subcontracted, or be arranged for transport to any 3rd party without the prior written consent of the
Dispatch.
GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTES
This agreement shall be governed by and constructed in accordance with laws of the State of
without regard to the conflicts of laws. Any disputes arising from this agreement shall be resolved
before the courts of the same State.
COUNTERPARTS
This agreement may be executed in two or more counterparts each of which shall be deemed an
original and all of which together shall constitute one and the same agreement.
NON-MODIFICATION
Any modifications made to this agreement shall only be considered as agreed by the parties in the
existence of a written instrument signed by both parties.
SEPARABILITY
Should any provision of this Agreement be held invalid by any competent court, the same shall apply
only to the provision affected while the remaining provisions hereto shall remain valid and
enforceable.
